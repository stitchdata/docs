To create the IAM policy: 1. In AWS, [navigate to the IAM service](https://console.aws.amazon.com/iam/home#/home){:target="new"} by clicking the **Services** menu and typing **IAM**. 2. Click **IAM** once it displays in the results. 3. On the IAM home page, click **Policies** in the menu on the left side of the page. 4. Click **Create Policy**. 5. In the **Create Policy** page, click the **JSON** tab. 6. Select everything currently in the text field and delete it. 7. In the text field, paste the Stitch IAM policy. 8.
