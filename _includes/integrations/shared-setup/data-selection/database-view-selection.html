Setting a database view to replicate is similar to selecting a table, with a few differences. Refer to the [Replicating Database Views guide]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl }}) for detailed instructions. At a high level, you'll need to complete the following to select a database view: 1. [Verify the database user's permissions]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl | append: "#step--verify-database-user-permissions" }}) 2. [Select the view]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl | append: "#step--select-view-in-stitch" }}) 3. **Optional**: [Define the view's Primary Key]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl | append: "#step--define-view-primary-key" }}) 4. [Define the view's Replication Method]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl | append: "#step--define-view-replication-method" }}) 5. [Save the view's settings]({{ link.replication.db-views | prepend: site.baseurl | append: "#step--save-view-settings" }})
